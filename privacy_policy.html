Privacy Policy

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data collected when you use our services.

Data We Collect

We do not collect personal data such as your name, email address, phone number, or any information that directly identifies you. Instead, we generate a unique ID for your device when you first use our services. This unique ID is used for internal purposes such as usage analytics and service improvement.

Data Usage

We use the unique ID to:

  • Analyze usage: We analyze usage data to understand how our services are used and to improve their quality.
  • Provide services: The unique ID is used to identify your device and provide appropriate services.

Data Sharing with Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or disclose your unique ID to any third parties, except as required by law or to protect our rights and interests.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your unique ID from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you by email or through a notice on our services.
